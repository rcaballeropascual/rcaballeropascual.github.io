About me

FULL NAME Ricardo Caballero Pascual
CURRENT POSITION Game Designer
CURRENT EMPLOYERS AHEARTFULOFGAMES S.L.

Hello! My name is Ricardo, a game designer based in Spain who loves crafting new worlds and experimenting with new ideas.

My career began in 2016, when I began to study the Official Bachelor Degree in Game Design at U-tad (University of Technology, Arts and Design). It was there that I had the opportunity to learn the profession, make contacts, and evolve my own skills through the development of small prototypes and video games.

By the time I graduated with honors four years later, I had already published my first game on Steam along with the other members of Null Reference Studio. This game was "Burn Me Twice", a narrative-driven game where I held the position of narrative designer and game writer. "Burn Me Twice" was later recognized with numerous awards praising its simple but beautiful aesthetics, its complex world full of details and its direct and light-hearted story.

After that, I joined Drakhar Studio S.L. as a Game Designer, and since then I have been able to work on various projects, like PAW Patrol The Movie: Adventure City Calls or Hotel Transylvania: Scary-Tale Adventures, where I have honed my skills as a professional.

When I'm not designing or working on personal projects, I often spend time reading books and studying the folklore of other cultures, playing games or practicing my writing skills.

I'm not afraid to go on new adventures, constantly looking to expand my horizons.

Favorite Game: Resident Evil (Capcom, 1996)
