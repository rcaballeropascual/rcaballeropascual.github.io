PAW PATROL THE MOVIE:
ADVENTURE CITY CALLS

"Play as Chase, Skye, Marshall, Rubble, Rocky, Zuma or Liberty, and patrol the streets of Adventure City to stop Humdinger's evil plans"

"PAW Patrol The Movie: Adventure City Calls" is a 3D platform adventure game based on the movie of the same name "PAW Patrol: The Movie". Humdinger has become the mayor of Adventure City and the pups will have to race into action to stop his evil plans.

During the development of this project, I worked together with other designers to create and polish the levels of the game. Following the narrative established beforehand in a script, I focused mainly on converting these guidelines into the video game as fully playable levels.

Each level lasts approximately 15 minutes, and contains mini-games that provide varied gameplay to keep the player's attention at all times.

In addition, I was in charge of the implementation of the voices in the game, regardless of the language. I also tested and corrected important bugs before release, and I actively participated in the design and implementation of the endless runner mini-games.

The design of games for young children takes into account their cognitive characteristics to offer them affordable challenges. In this way we prevent them from getting frustrated and maximize their experience.

The player can play as Liberty, Marshall, Zuma, Rubble, Chase, Rocky or Skye.

This is a cooperative multiplayer videogame, so the levels are designed to work both in singleplayer mode and with two players.

In each level there are mini-games that momentarily change the gameplay to prevent the player from getting bored.

The objective of each level is simple: reach the goal by collecting as many pup treats as possible. However, there are platforming elements to add variety to the gameplay.

The game follows a brand-new narrative that takes place shortly after the events of the movie.

The player can enjoy a wide variety of mini-games and interactive events that can be played alone or with a friend.
